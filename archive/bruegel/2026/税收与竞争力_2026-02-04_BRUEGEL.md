# 税收与竞争力

## 中文摘要与研判

该专题项目研究欧盟税收政策和税务管理如何塑造竞争力，重点关注欧洲语境下竞争力的定义、投资激励、创新、数字化等新兴驱动因素，以及税收政策与贸易、投资环境之间的互动。其意义在于把税制视为创新和产业竞争力政策的一部分，而不只是财政收入工具。对后续知识库调用而言，该材料可作为“创新政策工具”样本，用于比较欧洲如何通过税收、投资激励和数字化转型安排支撑长期竞争力。

## English Source Material

One of the main objectives of the project is to examine how tax policy and tax administration shape European Union competitiveness. This includes exploring how competitiveness should be defined in the European context, assessing both traditional and emerging drivers of competitiveness, such as investment incentives, innovation and digitalisation, and analysing the interaction between tax policy and related areas, including trade and the broader investment environment. The research aims to generate actionable and evidence-based insights that support coherent, forward-looking and future-proof tax strategies for the EU and its member states.
