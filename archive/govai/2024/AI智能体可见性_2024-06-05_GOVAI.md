# AI智能体可见性

## 中文摘要与研判

本文讨论在商业、科研、政府和个人活动中更多委托 AI 智能体执行复杂目标后，如何建立对智能体使用情况的“可见性”。作者将可见性界定为关于 AI 智能体在何处、为何、如何以及由谁使用的信息，并提出三类提升可见性的措施：智能体标识符、实时监测和活动日志。文章还比较这些措施在集中式到去中心化部署场景中的适用性，并讨论其对隐私和权力集中的影响。

研判要点：该文与“AI系统标识符”和“AI智能体基础设施”形成连续议题，重点从身份识别扩展到运行过程可观测。对城市级 AI 应用治理而言，智能体可见性是责任追踪、事故调查、平台治理和高风险场景准入的前提，但也会带来隐私保护、数据留存边界和平台权力集中问题。后续可与上海政务 AI、金融交易、客服外呼和自动化采购场景联动研究。

## English Source Material

Visibility into AI Agents Increased delegation of commercial, scientific, governmental, and personal activities to AI agents—systems capable of pursuing complex goals with limited supervision—may exacerbate existing societal risks and introduce new risks. Understanding and mitigating these risks involves critically evaluating existing governance structures, revising and adapting these structures where needed, and ensuring accountability of key stakeholders. Information about where, why, how, and by whom certain AI agents are used, which we refer to as visibility, is critical to these objectives. In this paper, we assess three categories of measures to increase visibility into AI agents: agent identifiers, real-time monitoring, and activity logging. For each, we outline potential implementations that vary in intrusiveness and informativeness. We analyze how the measures apply across a spectrum of centralized through decentralized deployment contexts, accounting for various actors in the supply chain including hardware and software service providers. Finally, we discuss the implications of our measures for privacy and concentration of power.
