# AI系统标识符

## 中文摘要与研判

本文提出为 AI 系统实例配置标识符（IDs）的治理框架。随着 AI 系统广泛进入交易、沟通、服务和自动化决策场景，用户、调查者和监管者往往难以确认某个具体系统是否具备安全认证、由谁部署、发生事故后应由谁负责。文章借鉴航空器等领域的标识制度，主张为具体 AI 系统实例分配可查询的标识，并关联系统类别、部署者、认证状态和交互所需信息。作者认为，该机制在 AI 系统可能对现实世界产生重大影响的场景中尤其有必要。

研判要点：AI 系统标识符可被视为智能体时代的基础治理工具，连接身份认证、责任追踪、事故调查和公共信任。对城市级 AI 应用和产业监管而言，标识符机制有助于把抽象模型治理转化为可定位的运行实例治理，尤其适用于金融交易、公共服务、客服外呼、自动化采购和人机交互密集场景。

## English Source Material

IDs for AI Systems AI systems are increasingly pervasive, yet information needed to decide whether and how to engage with them may not exist or be accessible. A user may not be able to verify whether a system has certain safety certifications. An investigator may not know whom to investigate when a system causes an incident. It may not be clear whom to contact to shut down a malfunctioning system. Across a number of domains, IDs address analogous problems by identifying particular entities (e.g., a particular Boeing 747) and providing information about other entities of the same class (e.g., some or all Boeing 747s). We propose a framework in which IDs are ascribed to instances of AI systems (e.g., a particular chat session with Claude 3), and associated information is accessible to parties seeking to interact with that system. We characterize IDs for AI systems, provide concrete examples where IDs could be useful, argue that there could be significant demand for IDs from key actors, analyze how those actors could incentivize ID adoption, explore a potential implementation of our framework for deployers of AI systems, and highlight limitations and risks. IDs seem most warranted in settings where AI systems could have a large impact upon the world, such as in making financial transactions or contacting real humans.
