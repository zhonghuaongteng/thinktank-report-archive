# Anthropic AI攻击对网络安全基础策略的冲击

## 中文摘要与研判

该页是 CSET 对 The National Interest 评论文章的摘要和专家引述，主要呈现 CSET 研究员 Andrew Lohn 对 AI 辅助网络攻击的判断。材料认为，AI 辅助攻击的关键变化不在于使用了全新的攻击技术，而在于攻击者能够在失败后快速适应和规模化调整，从而削弱传统纵深防御策略。Lohn 强调，组织需要重新组合多层阻断、检测和响应能力，才能承受由 AI 驱动的自适应攻击。

该材料适合作为 AI 改变网络安全攻防平衡的趋势线索，而非完整研究报告引用。对科技创新支撑研究的意义在于，它提示数字基础设施、企业安全治理和 AI 应用扩散之间的相互制约：AI 提升生产效率的同时，也改变漏洞利用、攻击迭代和防御体系设计。对上海而言，可作为 AI 安全测评、网络安全产业、企业数字化转型风险和关键基础设施防护研究的辅助材料。

## English Source Material

CSET’s Andrew Lohn shared his expert perspective in an op-ed published by The National Interest . In the piece, he explains that AI-assisted hacking signals a deeper cybersecurity threat: not new tools, but the breakdown of core defenses like defense in depth against adaptive, large-scale attackers. Reflecting on the implications of AI-driven cyberattacks, Lohn writes, “Although AI-enabled attacks use familiar techniques, their ability to rapidly adapt after failure shifts the balance between attackers and defenders. Organizations must rethink cybersecurity by combining blocking and detection across multiple layers to withstand AI-driven threats.” Although AI-enabled attacks use familiar techniques, their ability to rapidly adapt after failure shifts the balance between attackers and defenders.
