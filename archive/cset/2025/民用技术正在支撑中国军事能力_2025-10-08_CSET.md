# 民用技术正在支撑中国军事能力

## 中文摘要与研判

该页是 CSET 对 Foreign Policy 评论文章的摘要和专家引述，核心来自 Sam Bresnick 与 Cole McFaul 对中国军民融合和 AI 国防采购的分析。材料指出，中国正在把民用技术创新更快地导入军事能力建设，通过更广泛的企业和研究机构网络提升人工智能与自主系统能力。该文还关联 CSET 报告《Pulling Back the Curtain on China’s Military-Civil Fusion》，其样本包括 2023 年 1 月至 2024 年 12 月发布的 2857 份 AI 相关国防合同授标公告，显示传统军工主体仍占主导，但非传统供应商和研究机构正在发挥重要作用。

对科技创新支撑研究的价值在于，它把民用创新生态、国防采购、AI 应用和研究机构网络放进同一条技术转化链。对上海而言，该材料适合作为军民两用技术边界、企业与科研机构参与战略技术供给、AI 与自主系统场景转化的风险线索；后续若需要页码级证据，应回到 CSET 对应军民融合报告，而不直接把该媒体摘要作为完整报告引用。

## English Source Material

Sam Bresnick and Cole McFaul shared their expert analysis in an op-ed published by Foreign Policy . In their piece, they examine how China is rapidly integrating civilian technological innovation into its military capabilities through a strategy known as military-civil fusion, aiming to outpace the United States in areas like artificial intelligence and autonomous systems. China is successfully broadening its defense ecosystem and drawing on a wider network of companies and research institutions to enhance its AI capabilities. Over time, such gains could eventually erode the U.S. military’s technological advantage over the PLA. Over time, such gains could eventually erode the U.S. military’s technological advantage over the PLA.” The article delves into their latest CSET report, “ Pulling Back the Curtain on China’s Military-Civil Fusion ,“ which draws on 2,857 AI-related defense contract award notices published between January 2023 and December 2024. It find that while China’s legacy defense sector players lead AI-related military procurement, an emerging set of nontraditional vendors and research institutions appears to play a consequential role.
