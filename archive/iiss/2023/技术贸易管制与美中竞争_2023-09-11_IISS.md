# 技术贸易管制与美中竞争

## 中文摘要与研判

IISS 将美中竞争的核心变量界定为先进技术主导权，尤其是人工智能、微电子和量子信息系统。美国通过出口管制、产业政策和多边技术生态排除机制限制中国获取先进技术；中国则试图在依赖半导体的关键技术中实现领先，并加快本土能力建设以突破外部技术壁垒。

该条目虽为 summary_only，但对判断技术管制、半导体生态和科技竞争政策具有直接价值。对上海相关研究而言，可作为观察技术贸易管制如何影响产业链、研发合作和关键技术自主化压力的背景材料。

## English Source Material

Competition for technological superiority is now a key aspect of the rivalry between China and the United States. Mastery of advanced technologies like artificial-intelligence, microelectronics and quantum-information systems will determine military and economic pre-eminence in the future. The US has imposed significant restrictions on China’s access to sophisticated technology, and it has adopted industrial policies and established multilateral frameworks to exclude China from certain technology ecosystems. Meanwhile, China aims to achieve world leadership in semiconductor-dependent technologies and plans to accelerate its development of indigenous capabilities to overcome the technology barrier raised by the US.
