# LLM智能体能否选择并调用生物工具：初步生物安全评估

## 中文摘要与研判

该报告初步评估LLM智能体是否能够选择并调用生物学工具，关注工具使用能力对生物安全风险的放大效应。对科技治理的意义在于，风险评估不能只测试模型问答内容，还必须测试代理式工作流、外部工具调用和任务完成能力。

Research Published Jun 25, 2026 Download PDF Share on LinkedIn Share on X Share on Facebook Email Biological tools (BTs) could potentially be misused by malicious actors seeking to design novel biological weapons. In this report, the authors evaluate seven large language model (LLM) agents on their abilities to select and operate BTs. They find that LLM agents are capable of performing initial interactions with BTs, which could lower expertise barriers for actors seeking to use BTs. Targeted testing of agent design capabilities is warranted.
