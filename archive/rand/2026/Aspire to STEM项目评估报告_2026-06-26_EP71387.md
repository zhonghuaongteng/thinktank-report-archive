# Aspire to STEM项目评估报告

## 中文摘要与研判

该评估报告围绕 Aspire to STEM 项目，关注 STEM 学习、参与和人才管道建设。其价值在于把科技人才培养前移到教育参与和职业兴趣形成阶段，为上海和相关区域的青少年科技教育、创新人才储备与长期人力资本政策提供比较线索。

## English Source Material

Evaluated the Aspire to STEM programme, which funded bespoke CPD and STEM enrichment in disadvantaged English secondary schools, finding it slightly increased pupil progression to STEM A levels but had no effect on science teacher retention.
